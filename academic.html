Key Strategies of Social Design

Decorative Arts and the Object-Spatial Environment. RGHPU Bulletin
2025

The article proposes a typology of social design strategies, identifying three key approaches: local solutions to global challenges, improving quality of life, and ensuring social sustainability. Examined projects illustrate shared characteristics critical for achieving positive change and transforming social systems, such as co-design with stakeholders and consideration of the local context.

Social Design as an Umbrella Term: Definition and Its Key Characteristics

Decorative Arts and the Object-Spatial Environment. RGHPU Bulletin
2025

The article explores the concept of social design, its various definitions and basic characteristics. The author argues that social design is an umbrella term for a number of socially-oriented design practices, based on practical common tools. The article describes the distinctive features of social design, reflecting its humanistic valuesdriven origin.
